Resume Building Workshop

Victors Club held its first Resume Building Workshop at Dronacharya College, led by AWS expert Prof. Pooja Khot. The session focused on crafting strong resumes and improving LinkedIn profiles. Outreach Program

Victors Club hosted an "Introduction to Software and AI" session, introducing government school students to software, web development, and AI. Led by Ankit Sahu and Aditya Mahlawat, the event featured hands-on learning, a tech quiz, and support from Prof. Pooja Khot. We're committed to advancing education and technology!
