What is
VICTORS ?

VICTORS is a dynamic technical club focused on empowering students with skills in AWS, Cloud Computing, and Android Development. Our goal is to turn innovative ideas into reality by providing hands-on experience with the latest cloud technologies and mobile application development. We foster a community where members can collaborate, learn, and grow together in the ever-evolving tech world.
